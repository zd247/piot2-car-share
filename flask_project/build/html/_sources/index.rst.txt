Welcome to Car Apllication's documentation!
===========================================

Welcome to the guide of Car Apllication website. 
If you are looking to book a car with automatic services then you have come to the right place! 
Here we have all the information about the website.

1. Who is this website for?

You don't need to come to the car agent to book a car.
You can just do everything on this website.
Every information about our cars is shown on the website.

2. What services do we have?

- We have online booking service for customer.
- Our systems are very modern and everything is automatic.
- Our cars have smart doorlocks that can synchronize with your devices.
- Our engineers are able to track the cars using GPS when the cars break down. 

Here we will cover every information you need about our website, our automatic services.
