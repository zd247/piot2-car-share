Intro to the Customer Role
==========================

This section show you how to use this website as a customer.

.. figure:: /images/customer/customer_home.png
    :align: center

    *The Customer Home Page*

Navigation
----------
1. Click the **Open** button on the top left.
2. Click on the route you want to go
    * Home: Go to the Home Page.
    * View Cars: View all cars, search info, book a car.
    * View History: View all booking history, search info.
    * Logout: Logout your acount.
    
3. Click the **x** button to close the Navigation.

View All Cars
-------------
1. After clicking **View Cars**, you will be redirected to this page.

.. figure:: /images/customer/customer_car.png
    :align: center

    *The Customer's Car Viewing Page*

2. The top search box is to search any keyword.
3. The search boxes at the end of the table is to search for specific info in every column.
4. Select the **Available** button to book a car. Booked car will show **Booked**.

Book A Car
----------

.. figure:: /images/customer/customer_book.png
    :align: center

    *The Customer's Car Booking Page*

1. After clicking **Available** you will be redirected to this page.
2. Your email and car name will show at the top.
3. Enter your information. Invalid dates will receive warnings.
4. Select **Book** to book. You will be redirected to home page.
5. Select **Cancel** to cancel. You will return to the Car View Page.

View History
------------
1. After clicking **View History**, you will be redirected to this page.

.. figure:: /images/customer/customer_history.png
    :align: center

    *The Customer's History Page*

2. The top search box is to search any keyword.
3. The search boxes at the end of the table is to search for specific info in every column.

Cancel Booking
--------------
1. You must book a car first.
2. When you go to the Home Page, you will see the message with current booking car's name.

.. figure:: /images/customer/customer_cancel.png
    :align: center

    *The Customer's Car Home Page With Message*
    
3. Click on the **Cancel Booking** button to cancel booking.
4. You will be redirected to Home Page without the message.
